What We Need From You

This is an exploration, not a final product. We want your reactions.

  1. Color palettes: Which 1-2 palettes feel right for Caring with Grace? Any that feel completely wrong?
  2. Fonts: Which pairing feels most "us"? Remember these need to work in email signatures too.
  3. Logo directions: Which 1-2 approaches should we develop further? Fine to mix ideas (e.g., "I like the butterfly from A but the layout of D").
  4. Overall mood: Should the brand feel more classic/traditional or modern/fresh?

Don't worry about getting it perfect — this round is about narrowing directions. We'll refine your top picks in the next round.
